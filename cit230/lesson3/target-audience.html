Target Audience

This site is purposely for the inhabitants of Eastern Idaho as well as travellers coming in and out of Idaho for its tourist attractions such as; Grand Teton National Park, Yellowstone, Bearworld and others. This site does not exclude farmers,students and families living in Boise, Soda Springs,and Twin Falls

Scenarios

  • Four BYU-Idaho students heading to "That Preston Rodeo Night" and their rest-stop is Lava hot spring.
  • An Idaho photographer wondering how the weather would be like in the next couple of days to go spot-scouting for a photoshoot.
  • A BYU-I student driving from Twin Falls in the morning to rexburg for classes from 9:45am - 4:45pm and wondering how the weather would be like.
